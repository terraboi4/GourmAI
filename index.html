👨‍🍳 KitchenGenie

If you're looking for a way to spice up your cooking routine, you're gonna want to use KitchenGenie. This digital service provides users with recipes based on the ingredients they have in their fridges. All you need is an internet connection. KitchenGenie goes beyond traditional cookbooks, too. The AI behind the service can also recommend dishes based on your dietary restrictions or preferences. So whether you're in a hurry or want something new, KitchenGenie is a great option.
